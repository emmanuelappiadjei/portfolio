DEF. — Beats x Deftones

A speculative brand collaboration concept — reimagining a limited-edition Beats collection around the visual and emotional world of Deftones.

Role

Creative Concept & Campaign Strategy

Focus

Brand Campaign, Product Concept, Social Strategy

Project Type

Speculative Collaboration Concept

DEF. x Beats by Dre by Emmanuel Appiadjei

The Vision

Deftones are one of rock's most influential bands, known for blending crushing intensity with atmospheric, emotionally charged sound. This collaboration is more than a product drop — it's an experience that lets fans celebrate their connection to the band and the impact its music has had on their lives. DEF. is designed not only for devoted rock fans, but for anyone who wants to wear what they love like a badge of honor.

Brand Positioning

Pair Beats by Dre's premium sound and bass performance with Deftones' deafening intensity, layered harmonies, and atmospheric sound — positioning the headphones as a physical expression of fandom and music culture, not just a listening device.

Target Audience

The 20–30-year-old nostalgia-driven music fan — someone who treats the artists they follow as part of their personal identity and style, not just a genre they listen to.

Product Collection

Three headphone editions, each drawing its design language from a different era of the band's catalog:

  • White Pony — minimal, surreal, sharp and high-contrast
  • Diamond Eyes — dreamlike, luminous, atmospheric
  • Private Music — hazy, spacious, and introspective, drawing on the album's white-snake motif of shedding and rebirth

Each edition includes an etched album title, a numbered limited-edition plate, custom ear-cup graphics, and a matching carrying case — packaged like a collectible album release rather than standard electronics: translucent sleeve, distressed typography, a numbered collector card, and a QR code unlocking the edition's curated playlist.

Social & Digital Strategy

Content leans cinematic, moody, and fan-made rather than overly polished — think grainy tour footage, slow-motion product reveals, and a "Choose Your Era" carousel styled like an old music blog. Each edition pairs with a curated Deftones playlist, an AR filter, and a "Which Deftones Era Are You?" quiz to extend the campaign beyond the product itself.

Experiential Activation

A launch pop-up with separate listening rooms for each album — visitors pick an edition, try its matching headphones, and walk away with a digital or physical collector card. A companion concept: a merch booth at Deftones concerts where fans can test headphones, customize a carrying case, and unlock a concert-exclusive color.
