Project Overview

HFMA Annual 2026 was one of Revecore’s major event activations, centered around a private suite experience, partner receptions, targeted event communication, sales support, and post-event follow-up. My work supported both the operational side of the event and the marketing pieces that helped drive attendance, engagement, and internal alignment.

My Contributions

  • Supported email marketing execution by helping format, build out, and review event-related emails for accuracy, layout, and overall presentation.
  • Assisted with promotional support for partner receptions, helping organize details that encouraged attendance and supported relationship-building opportunities.
  • Helped support planning and organization for the HFMA Annual private suite experience, including event details, internal coordination, and on-site preparation materials.
  • Created and organized internal resources, including important contact lists, team details, and planning documents to help the team stay aligned before the event.
  • Supported invite, RSVP, and attendance tracking needs for suite and reception-related activity.
  • Helped prepare and ship event materials, booth items, and related supplies to support a smooth event experience.
  • Assisted with post-event feedback by helping build survey materials, gather responses, and organize insights for future event planning.
  • Supported lead list organization and follow-up preparation after the event.

Marketing Impact

The event helped support meaningful prospect and client conversations through a mix of private meeting space, partner receptions, targeted communication, and follow-up. One reception I helped promote contributed to a $1.5M closed deal, showing how event marketing and relationship-driven experiences can influence pipeline and business outcomes.

Creative Marketing Takeaways

This project helped me better understand how event marketing goes beyond logistics. The strongest event experiences depend on clear communication, thoughtful promotion, audience targeting, branded touchpoints, and follow-up that turns in-person engagement into measurable business value.
