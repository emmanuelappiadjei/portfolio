Collectible, not just packaging.

Each edition ships in a translucent sleeve with distressed typography, a numbered collector card, and a QR code that unlocks the edition's curated playlist — designed to feel like an album release, not an electronics box.

Beyond the box.

Every edition pairs with a curated Deftones playlist, an AR filter, and a "Which Deftones Era Are You?" quiz — extending the collection into a digital experience fans can carry with them.
